Help & Instructions
How to use the Price Tracker:
  1. Add New Price Entry: Fill in the product details, supplier name, price, date, and quantity/unit.
  2. View Records: All your price entries will appear in the table and chart.
  3. Filter Data: Use the filters to view specific products, suppliers, or date ranges.
  4. Edit/Delete: Click the action buttons to modify or remove entries.
  5. Export Data: Export your price history for record-keeping.
Tips for Best Use:
  • Record prices regularly to track trends accurately
  • Use consistent product names for better filtering
  • Compare prices from different suppliers before making purchases
  • Watch the price chart to identify seasonal price changes
